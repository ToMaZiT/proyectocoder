Game trailer

Everything is going to be okay.

It is a video game in the psychological and surreal horror genre that explores themes such as anxiety, depression and trauma. The player controls a hikikomori boy, named Sunny, and his alter-ego in the dream world, Omori; who explore both worlds with the aim of overcoming their fears and discovering secrets: with emotions at the central point of the narrative.

Explore a strange world full of colorful friends and foes. When the time comes, the path you’ve chosen will determine your fate... and perhaps the fate of others as well.
